Credit Insurance is a protection against losses due to defaults. Its essential function is to preserve cash flow and provide confidence in international transactions.

This service can contemplate all or part of the insured company’s credits and considers loss history in addition to portfolio risk. Credit limits are always based on the ability of the insured company’s clients to pay.
