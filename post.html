Young Talents Project

The 2nd Edition of Apprentice Worldwide 2019 in Brazil was a great success!

The event took place last Friday, April12, in the head office of Way Back from 6pm to 9pm, and the main theme of the year was “The Importance of Global Networking in Business”.

On a non-profit basis, Apprentice Worldwide was created and founded by Mr. Hernan Mayol in January of 2014 in Miami, USA, and it is now present in 12 countries.

It promotes the sharing of experiences between executives and young talents worldwide, and aims to contribute to the success in their professional careers in the future.

h3 - title

The project is mediated by a mentor (a successful executive) – nowadays there are 57 mentors around the world – in an intense exchange of knowledge and experiences in oder to prepare the future leaders of tomorrow.

Our Director of Human Resources and Vice President of Way Back, Ms. Marcia Viana, enthusiast and a great supporter and volunteer in this worldwide project in Brazil, opened the event talking about the the importance of Networking towards the success in the business , and sharing her own experiences in how to forge strong corporate relationships.

Afterwards, we called upon Mr. Jefferson Frauches Viana, President of Way Back and stockholder of the biggest conglomerate of Credit & Collection in the world – TCM Group, Mr. Erick Oliveira, Director of Operations of Way Back and Mr. Ricardo Costa, Manager of Legal and International Affairs of Way Back, to talk about their professional careers and how essential Networking was to achieve success.
